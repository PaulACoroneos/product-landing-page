About

Alexander's Ragtime Band is a jazz band that specializes in playing old time jazz. We play at all sorts of events from weddings to parties to school events. We even play on the street! We are based in New York City but are willing to travel to play at your event. We have been playing together for over 20 years and have a combined experience of over 100 years. We are the best at what we do and we love to play music!
